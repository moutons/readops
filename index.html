ReadOps

A virtual bookclub for people interested in tech ops

Welcome to ReadOps! We're still getting organized, but let's get started anyway.

For May, we're going to read Mark Burgess's In Search of Certainty. Here's a (proposed) schedule:

  • finish Part 1: Stability by May 10th,
  • finish Part 2: Certainty by May 17th,
  • finish Part 3: Promises by May 24th, and
  • meet in a Hangout to discuss in person on Thursday, May 29th (so that we don't conflict with HangOps).

The general idea is to discuss the book via the Google Group through the month, then all get together at the end to chat and wrap things up.

Suggestions (for schedule, format, and the next book) are welcome!
